class administrative_area.AdministrativeArea(country_name: str, admin_level: int)

Get the boundaries of administrative areas for a specified country.

The administrative area level is specified by an integer, where 0 is the country level, 1 is the next broadest level (e.g. states or provinces), and so on.

It is possible to get the geometry of a specific administrative area by name.

Example usage:

`python timor_leste = AdministrativeArea("Timor-Leste", admin_level=1) print(timor_leste.get_admin_area_names()) baucau_boundaries = timor_leste.get_admin_area_boundaries("Baucau") timor_leste_country_code = timor_leste.get_iso3_country_code() `
